Mission Statement


The purpose of this project is to analyze California housing data over the last 10 years. We will examine the trends for the past three years in order to provide an in depth analysis that specifically demonstrates the impact in the housing market during the years of COVID. We employed a machine learning model to predict future trends for the coming years. Hosting the flask app.
